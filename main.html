Plot Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased if he can implant an idea into a target's subconscious, a process called inception. As Cobb and his team dive deeper into dreams within dreams, the line between reality and the dream world blurs.
