Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of extracting valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform "inception" — the act of planting an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, alongside an ensemble cast including Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Marion Cotillard.
